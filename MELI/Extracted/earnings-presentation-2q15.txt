                                • MP On-platform penetration 57%, up 23 pp YoY, with Brazil over 90%

Continued progress on           • MP Off-platform: TPV increasing 108% YoY in LC and 73% in USD
 strategic initiatives          • MercadoEnvíos: Shipped over 40% of sold items in Brazil, Argentina, Mexico and Colombia.

                                • Official Stores: close to 1,000 brands and large retailers selling through MELI’s marketplace
